# Shell

The shape is somewhat like the Pocket Pikachu, a rectangular egg-like shape, but with the bottom-right corner dipping downward, giving it a unique look. This corner has a through-hole for phone straps that passes through the back and bottom; many official straps were released as PM merch.

The console comes in three colors: Wooper Blue, Chikorita Green, and Smoochum Purple. In the [Pokémon Channel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_Channel) GameCube game, Gold and [Wood](https://gyazo.com/7fc13d58e38968ac40b37e56ce99d768) colors are also available.

## Screws

The battery screw cannot be safely removed from the cover.

### Case screws

Four screws, one at each corner of the device. There is no security tape or stickers covering any screws.

* 6.6mm long
* 1.4mm width over threads
* 0.9mm head thickness
* 8 threads
* Zinc Dichromate plating
* 3.0 (Y1) tri-wing
* pan head?

### Board screws

Three screws, two located on the middle-left side of the board and one in the bottom-right.

* 6.5mm long
* 1.5mm width over threads
* 1.0mm head thickness
* 7 threads
* Black Zinc plating
* 0 philips
* pan head?

### IR screws

Two screws which screw through the board and the plastic IR cover into the front shell.

* 5.8mm long
* 1.5mm width over threads
* 3.2mm head width
* 0.7mm top of head to first thread
* 0.2mm head thickness
* Zinc Dichromate plating
* 0 philips
* flat head notebook(?) screw
