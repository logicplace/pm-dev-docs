# Flash carts

Flash carts are special cartridges with allow you to flash software onto them which can run on real PM hardware. Each options offers different features and has different pros and cons.

Most flash carts require a custom linker, also called a flasher or programmer (or progger), in order to write to the cartridge from your PC.

WARNING: When writing to a flash cart, you need to either remove the cartridge from your PM or remove the battery from your PM to ensure that the cartridge slot is unpowered.

## Currently available

* [PM2040](./pm2040.md): 2 or 16 MiB with multicart support
* [PokMini](./pokmini.md): 2 MiB with*out* multicart support
* [DITTO mini](./ditto.md): 2 MiB with multicart support
* [Leon·DIY](./leon_diy.md): China only, 512 KiB

## Previously available
